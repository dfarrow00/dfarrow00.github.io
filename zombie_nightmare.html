Zombie Nightmare


Itch.io page

Zombie Nightmare is a first-person zombie wave shooter created in Unity where the player must survive as many waves of zombies as possible to achieve a high score. Each round, the amount of zombies and their spawn rates increase. Every two rounds, the player is presented with two randomly generated forfeit cards. The player must select one of these cards which will have a negative effect for the duration of the game (e.g. reduced health, increased zombie damage, etc.).
